What does the name [Sanppo] mean?

The word [Sanppo] is a pronunciation of korean word [산보] and japanese word [散歩]. Same pronunciation, Same meaning.

In english, it means [Stroll] or [Walk]

We wish to work like [Sappo] with contemplation. We wish to make useful, comfortable and joyful things like [Sappo]
